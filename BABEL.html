BABEL — an arcade space shooter.

Design, code, art & sound: Claude & You

Built with HTML5 Canvas, the Web Audio API, and a lot of vector triangles pretending to be starships.

Inspired by the golden age of arcade shooters — Galaga, Space Invaders, Sky Force, and Raiden.

No aliens were harmed in the making of this game. Several thousand were vaporized.
